Enterprise Performance.
Local Sovereignty.

Zenith runs on cutting-edge hardware and uses state-of-the-art architecture – for speed and reliability you can feel.

Optimised for Minisforum MS S1 Max

Zenith runs on the compact Intel-based mini PC with up to 128 GB DDR5 RAM, fast NVMe storage and a quiet fan system – ideal for continuous office operation.
